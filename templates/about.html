About the UK Immigration Data Visualiser

This project provides an interactive map to explore UK visa statistics from 2005 to 2025. The dataset is taken from the official gov.uk page. You can download the dataset here.

Simply go to the Immigration Map page, select a year, quarter, and application outcome (issued, refused, withdrawn) from the dropdown menus, then click on a country you would like to explore to see a detailed breakdown of visa types in the pie chart.
